# CardKeeper

CardKeeper is a secure Android application for managing your financial accounts, identity documents, and more.

## Key Features

- **Secure Storage**: Keep your card and bank details in one place.
- **Card Network Logos**: Automatic brand recognition for Visa, Mastercard, and more.
- **Quick Copy**: Easily copy account and card numbers to your clipboard.
- **Automatic Caching**: High-performance logo loading with offline support.

## Getting Started

1. **Download the App**: Go to the [Releases](https://github.com/evijayan2/CardKeeper/releases) page and download most recent alpha APK.
2. **Install**: Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" on your Android device and open the APK.
3. **Add Your Cards**: Start adding your financial and identity documents!
