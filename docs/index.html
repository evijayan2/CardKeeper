CardKeeper

Securely manage your financial accounts, identity documents, and more.

Download Latest Alpha Release

Key Features

  • Secure Storage: Keep your card and bank details in one place.
  • Card Network Logos: Automatic brand recognition for Visa, Mastercard, and more.
  • Quick Copy: Easily copy account and card numbers to your clipboard.
  • Automatic Caching: High-performance logo loading with offline support.

Getting Started

  1. Download the latest .apk from the Releases page.
  2. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" on your device.
  3. Install and start adding your cards!

Privacy

CardKeeper is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device.
